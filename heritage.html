A Legacy of Excellence

For three decades, we have been crafting exceptional diamond jewelry, transforming the rarest stones into timeless masterpieces.

The Atelier

Where Masterpieces Are Born

In our atelier, master craftsmen transform rough diamonds into works of art. Each piece passes through skilled hands — from the initial sketch to the final polish — guided by a pursuit of perfection that has defined our house for three generations.

Every creation is a dialogue between the gem and the artisan, where centuries-old techniques meet contemporary vision. The result is not merely jewelry, but heirlooms destined to transcend generations.

Our Journey

Three Decades of Craft

From a small workshop to a house known for exceptional diamond craftsmanship.

1995

Shree Giriraj Gems and Jewels was founded with a vision to create exceptional diamond jewelry.

2000

Established our signature manufacturing unit with master craftsmen, laying the foundation for our atelier tradition.

2010

Expanded our collection to include international clientele and bespoke services, earning recognition for exceptional craftsmanship.

2020

Launched our digital presence while deepening our commitment to handmade craftsmanship and ethical sourcing.

Present

A house defined by the pursuit of perfection — where every creation carries the legacy of our founders and the skill of our master artisans.

Our Philosophy

We Don't Just Create Jewelry

At Shree Giriraj, we believe that fine jewelry is more than an ornament — it's a legacy. Each piece we create is meant to be treasured, passed down, and worn with pride across generations.

Our approach combines traditional craftsmanship with contemporary aesthetics, ensuring every creation is both timeless and relevant.
